Privacy Policy

This application is a personal-use tool built by a single developer (Jonathan Hicks / GitHub: jshicks) to retrieve and analyze his own WHOOP data.

Data retrieved from the WHOOP API — including recovery, sleep, workout, cycle, and profile data — is stored locally on the developer's own device. It is not shared with, sold to, transmitted to, or processed by any third party or external service.

This application is not distributed to or used by any other individuals. Only the developer's own WHOOP account is connected to this application.

You may revoke this application's access to your WHOOP data at any time from your WHOOP account settings.
